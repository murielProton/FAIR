Warning

This is the list of all the french bank holidays. If you change these make sure you also change the CONSTANT in UTIL_Duration.cls : NUMBER_OF_MS_IN_ONE_YEAR. For the record, a year is 253 work day, and a day is 8,5 hours or 30 600 000 milliseconds.

Accounts, Teams and rôles can be set to add specific holydays.

At the end of 2022 make sure these days are recuring in 2023. Also find out the new dates for :All Saints' Day, Ascension day, and Easter.
