Major BUG

Imediataly after installing your new app, creating a new record on sObject agf__ADM_Work__c, will be impossible. It is due to an endless loop in the creation procees of a new reccord on agf__ADM_Work__c. A new record Work needs a 'Product Tag', which needs a Team. And a Team needs at least two Team Membesr to exist.

Work ← Product Tag ← Team ←→ 2 Team Members ← Users

Step 1

Go to the Setup of this organasation.

1- Go to the layouts of agf__ADM_Work__c :

Make sure the only required fields are the folowing :

  • Subject
  • Product Tag
  • Priority
  • Status
  • Found in Build

2- Go to the layouts of agf__ADM_Scrum_Team__c :

Make sure the only required field is the folowing :

  • Team Name

3- Go to the layouts of agf__ADM_Product_Tag__c :

Make sure the only required fields are the folowing :

  • Product Tag Name
  • Team

4- Go to the layouts of agf__ADM_Scrum_Team_Member__c :

Make sure the only required field is the folowing :

  • Team

Step 2

In you organasation create :

  • 2 Teams Members
  • at least one Team
  • at least one Product Tag
  • finaly a record Work

While you create thoese new reccord, you will notice that some fields are required but are not stamped with a red star. Take notes and go to each layouts and check the boxes 'required' for each 'real' mandatory fields.
