Business Hours

In the setup of your org, go click on the tab 'Home'. In quick find search for Business Hours. Next to default record, click on Edit. Do not forget tot set your time zone on the right seting, for this project I chose : (GMT+01:00) Central European Standard Time (Europe/Paris). To complete the table use numbers and letters. If you want a day off fill in : HH:MM to HH:MM. If you enter any other keys you will end up with a 21h shift on your off day. Trust me, I've tried.
